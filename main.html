LAKE CAVANAUGH IMPROVEMENT ASSOCIATION

Lake Cavanaugh Improvement Association is a Washington non-profit association founded in 1953 for the following purposes:

To promote a spirit of friendship and cooperation amongst Lake Cavanaugh property owners and residents

To provide for a better understanding of a means for solving problems and achieving goals common to all

To provide a means for mutual protection against any influences considered detrimental to the Lake Community
